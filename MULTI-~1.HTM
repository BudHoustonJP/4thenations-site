01

Stability before expansion

Jesus did not cross to the Decapolis until the Galilean movement was multiplying through his trained disciples. Productivity at the primary node is what affords the secondary excursion.

02

Both ditches are real

Expanding too early stalls both nodes. Staying entrenched buries the talent. Depth that never generates movement is not faithfulness.

03

The non-residential spark

In the Decapolis, Jesus heals one man, gives him one mandate ('Go home and tell'), and exits fast before becoming a dependency. He returns later to a Gentile crowd of 4,000.

Mark 5 to 7, the feeding of the 4,000
04

Forced exits, phase-specific letters

Paul spent as little as three weeks in Thessalonica before a forced exit. He managed the node remotely with letters and an envoy. The Epistles were phase-specific interventions.

3 weeks in Thessalonica (Acts 17)
05

The Phase 6 hub

Three years in Ephesus built a regional hub that formed and released local insiders like Epaphras to reach cities Paul never visited. He aimed for a reproducing network.

3 years, 'all Asia heard' (Acts 19:10)
06

Spain: when the model must change

'No place left' in the east was an assessment of network capacity. Spain was a Phase 0 frontier where the synagogue and Roman road would not be waiting. The model is not automatic at the frontier.

Romans 15
07

Three tests: pattern or principle?

A pattern becomes a binding principle only if it passes three tests: repetition across actors, theological grounding in God's nature, and adaptability into new cultural forms.

Repetition, Grounding, Adaptability
08

Same principle, new form

A hand-carried letter and a WhatsApp coaching call do the same work. The mistake is treating the form as binding; the bigger mistake is assuming its absence means the principle is gone.

“Depth that never generates movement is not faithfulness. It is a buried talent.”

“A WhatsApp group of village leaders and the Ephesian network run on the same logic.”

The logic was the strategy. When a pattern passes the three tests, it reproduces.
