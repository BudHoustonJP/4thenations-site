From creators to consumers

We changed the verb

One word decides whether faith is something you build or something you attend. Toggle between the two and watch the posture shift.

Create: a vocation you inhabit

Faith as a craft. You show up to make something, to serve, to build a people. Belonging is produced by what you give, so absence is felt by the whole body. The question is what can I offer here.

A maker rarely asks to be entertained.

The slow, un-novel work

Roughly seven in ten departures trace back to causes a sender could have addressed before deploying anyone.

It is neurological, not only spiritual: the dopamine loop craves novelty, and missions is slow and un-novel.
